Key Insights
Organic Discovery is Working

Nearly 25% of streams came from Search — people are actively looking for this track. Another 23% from playlists, and 28% from Collections (saves/libraries). With zero promotion, that's a strong signal the concept resonates.

January Spike = Untapped Momentum

~200 streams in January suggests an organic burst — possibly a share, feature, or playlist add. Investigate what happened in January to replicate it intentionally for the EP launch.

Steep Drop-Off Feb–Apr

Streams fell from ~200 in January to ~40 in Feb/Mar and just 2 so far in April. No promotion = no sustained growth. The EP launch on April 9 needs a campaign behind it or the same pattern will repeat.

US is Your Core Market

218 of ~228 tracked streams are from the US. Switzerland and Spain show early international reach. Lean into the US audience first for the EP campaign — then target UK and Africa as secondary markets given the STEAM education angle.

Playlist Placement is the Priority

23% playlist discovery with no active outreach means curator submissions post-release could meaningfully move the needle. Start SubmitHub and Indiemono submissions April 14 once the EP has some streams to show.
